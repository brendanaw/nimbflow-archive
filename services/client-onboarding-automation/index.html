How the OnboardFlow™ client onboarding system works

Automate every messy handoff from intake to kickoff so projects start clean, fast, and fully aligned.

Instant client confirmation & welcome

Clients get a branded confirmation, thank-you note, and clear next steps the second payment lands. No more clients left in limbo.

  • Triggers instant emails or any other communication
  • Includes personalized next step without guessing or silence
  • Kicks off onboarding before anyone manually replies

Example tools we use: Stripe, Make, Gmail, Slack

Frictionless info capture

Collect all key info with a clean, client-friendly form that routes data into your tools automatically with zero email ping-pong.

  • Dynamic intake forms tailored to each service or client
  • Routes data to Airtable, Notion, ClickUp, or your CRM
  • Notifies your team instantly without manual sorting

Example tools we use: Tallyform, Airtable, Make, Notion, ClickUp

Consolidate & Start

Auto-create folders, docs, and kickoff assets

System auto-builds all project folders, kickoff docs, and onboarding assets so your team never scrambles or copies templates by hand.

  • Folders and files spun up for every new client
  • Templates duplicated and pre-filled
  • Shared links and access sent to your team and the client
